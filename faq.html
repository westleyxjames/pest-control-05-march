Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pest control services

For residential properties, we recommend quarterly pest control services (every 3 months) to maintain a pest-free environment. However, the frequency can vary depending on your specific situation, the severity of pest activity, and the type of pests. Some properties may benefit from monthly service, while others may only need bi-annual treatments. During your free inspection, we'll assess your property and recommend the most effective service schedule for your needs.
Yes, absolutely! The safety of your family and pets is our top priority. We use only EPA-approved products that are safe when applied correctly. Our technicians are trained to apply treatments in a manner that minimizes exposure. For most treatments, pets and children can return to treated areas once the products have dried, typically within 2-3 hours. We'll provide specific safety instructions based on the treatment method used. If you have specific concerns about allergies or sensitivities, please let us know and we can discuss alternative treatment options.
Yes, we offer same-day service for most pest control needs, subject to availability. If you call us before noon, we can often schedule a technician to visit your property the same day. For emergency situations such as bee swarms, wasp nests near entryways, or severe rodent infestations, we prioritize scheduling and will do our best to accommodate you as quickly as possible. Contact us at (323) 446-0886 to check same-day availability.
The duration of treatment depends on the size of your property and the type of service being performed. A typical residential pest control treatment takes between 30 minutes to 1 hour for interior and exterior application. More extensive services like termite inspections or rodent exclusion work may take 2-3 hours. Commercial properties or severe infestations may require more time. Our technician will provide you with an estimated time frame when they arrive and keep you informed throughout the process.
Yes! We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If pests return between scheduled services, we'll come back and re-treat your property at no additional charge. Our guarantee covers the pests included in your service plan. For most general pest control services, we guarantee protection against common household pests for up to 90 days. Specialized services like termite treatment come with their own specific guarantees. We'll provide you with detailed guarantee information for your specific service.
Preparation requirements vary depending on the type of service. For general pest control, minimal preparation is needed - just ensure our technician has access to all areas that need treatment. For interior treatments, we recommend storing food, covering pet dishes, and removing pets during application. For bed bug treatments, specific preparation is required including washing bedding and removing clutter. We'll provide you with detailed preparation instructions when you schedule your service. If you have any questions about preparation, our office staff is happy to help.

There's no obligation to proceed with service after the inspection. The inspection typically takes 20-30 minutes, and you'll receive a detailed quote before any work is performed. Call us at (323) 446-0886 or fill out our online form to schedule your free inspection.
